Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy?

We ship throughout Germany, Austria, Netherlands and France. Shipping times typically range from 3-7 business days depending on your location.

Are your candles eco-friendly?

Absolutely! We use natural soy wax, cotton wicks, and eco-friendly packaging materials in all our candles.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping within Germany typically takes 3-5 days. Shipping to other EU countries may take longer.

What is your return policy?

As a small business, we pour a lot of care into each handcrafted item. We do not accept returns unless an item is defective or damaged upon arrival.

What should I do if my order is damaged?

In the rare event that your candle arrives damaged, please contact us immediately. Include your order number and a photo of the damaged item. We will be happy to assist you with a replacement or a refund.

Are your decorative candles only for decoration?

No, our decorative candles are designed to be burned just like our other candles. We use the same high-quality wax and wicks, so they will burn cleanly and beautifully.
